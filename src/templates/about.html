Workout tracker is built by Kai Sawamoto as his final project for CPSC101

It keeps track of your workout records and generates graphs to show your growth of Big3 exercises - bench press, dead lift and squat

It also suggests weight, reps and sets for your Big3 exercises. It adopts the principle of nonlinear periodization and the the suggestion varies every time in a cycle of 5 days
